Food Allergies

Food Allergies should be taken with extreme caution when consuming certain foods. It is one of the most common fatal accidents among teens. One of the most fatal allergic reactions is anaphylaxis. Always check with your doctor on any allergies. Here are the most common food allergies:

  • milk
  • peanuts
  • eggs
  • soy
  • wheat
  • fish

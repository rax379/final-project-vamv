Smart Snacking

Are you a teenager with many after school activities such as football or cheerleadering? Do you get so hungry that you don't care what you eat pizza or chips? Let me help you out. There are solutions to avoid this unhealthy trap. How about some smart snacking to keep in track to the right healthy lifestyle. Below are some examples:there are healthy options to make it right. Here are some suggestions to keep smart snacking on track in your lifestyle:

  • Try creating your snacks ahead of time like making your trail mix, apples and peanut butter.
  • Choose alternatives foods such as baby carrots, granola bars, rice crackers, and low-fat yogurt that you can carry easily in your backpack.
  • Always check the serving size of your snack to avoid too many calories, sugar, or fat.

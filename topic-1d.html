Smart Snacking

Are you a teenager with many after school activities such as football or cheerleading? Do you get so hungry that you don't care if you eat pizza or chips? Let me help you out. There are solutions to avoid this unhealthy trap. How about some smart snacking to keep you in track to a healthy lifestyle? Below are some examples to help you stay on track to smart snacking in your lifestyle:

  • Try creating your snacks ahead of time like making your own trail mix, or an apples and peanut butter snack.
  • Choose alternatives foods such as baby carrots, granola bars, rice crackers, and low-fat yogurt that you can carry easily in your backpack.
  • Always check the serving size of your snack to avoid too many calories, sugar, or fat.

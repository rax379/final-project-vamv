Veterans and Memorial Day

Veterans Day Facts
  • Celebrated on November 11th.
  • Honors the war time service and sacrifice of men and women in the armed forces.
  • Began as Armstice Day, but renamed Veterans Day to honor veterans of all wars.
  • Organizations hold parades and ceremonies.
  • The President places a wreath on the Tomb of the Unknowns at the Arlington National Cemetery.


Memorial Day Facts
  • A day of prayer for peace.
  • 3pm National Moment of Remembrance
  • It became a federal holiday in 1971.
  • Celebrated on the last Monday of May
  • Honors the men and women who died in all wars

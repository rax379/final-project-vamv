There are many ways you can boost your daily activity. You can join a sports team in your school or find a non-competitive league in your city. You can also find a friend and start running or biking or even start going to a yoga class together. If you have a pet, be the one to take it for its daily walk. The important thing is to start moving more. It is a good idea to start with small steps and slowly move your way up to longer periods of physical activity.

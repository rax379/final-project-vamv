Thanksgiving Day and Independence Day

Thanksgiving Day Facts
  • Honors the early settlers and their harvest feast that they shared together.
  • A national day to give thanks.
  • Celebrated on the fourth Thursday of November.
  • Families have traditions with the food they eat (turkey, cranberry sauce, stuffing, etc.), who does the cooking, and what they do for the day.
  • It is often associated with over eating.


Independence Day Facts
  • One of the most celebrated holidays in the United States.
  • It is the day that Congress adopted the Declaration of Independence in 1776.
  • Celebrated on the fourth of July.
  • Many communities host parades with bands and patriotic speeches.
  • Red, white, and blue cover the nation and all through the night you can hear and possibly see fire work displays.

Physical Appearance

As you approach the teen years, it's common and natural for you to become more interested in appearances. Your body is going through some major changes. This is called puberty. As you change physically, you become more aware of how you look. The first step to a healthy physical appearance is to establish a working routine.
As a teen you need:

  • To shower daily. It is recommended that you use a mild soap and concentrate on your face, hands, feet, underarms, groin and bottom. Washing under the fingernails is important too.
  • Brushing your teeth twice a day is highly recommended as well.
  • Apply deodorant or antiperspirant regularly.

to keep a clean and healthy appearance.

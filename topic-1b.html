Reading Food Labels

Audio


Captions

Learning to read food labels correctly is an important skill to have in order to make smart choices in the supermarket and in restaurants. Food labels are filled with information that describes the contents of a product, and understanding this information is essential to help you make right choices. In this guide, you will learn how each section of a food label is the make-up of ingredients and nutrients and you will learn how these affect your diet. According to the FDA and USDA, the “average person’s diet” consists of 2,000 calories. Do you know the number of calories and the different ingredients that you consume daily? Are you reading food labels correctly?

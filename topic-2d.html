Losing Weight Safely

Audio


Captions

Talking about weight can sometimes be uncomfortable. However, being overweight can be a scary thing due to the many illnesses that are associated with excess weight. At your age, there are many changes happening to your body and your weight is going to be affected. You need to make healthy choices about your food and fitness levels to avoid having problems with your health. Instead of looking at scales and numbers and charts that tell you how much you should weigh, you need to see what you are doing to help maintain a good weight.

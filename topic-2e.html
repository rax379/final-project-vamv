Sleeping Patterns

Audio


Captions

Sleep is food for the brain. It is vital to your well-being and it is as important as the air you breathe, the water you drink and the food you eat. During sleep, important body functions and brain activity occur. It is important that you sleep the recommended 8-10 hours so that you can be alert and ready to tackle the day. Not getting enough sleep can cause more serious problems than just being sleepy. You will physically look bad, feel moody, and perform poorly in school and in sports.

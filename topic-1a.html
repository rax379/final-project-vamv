Portion Control will help understand the right amount of foods to eat in your daily diet. Although food labels have the number of calories and ingredients, it does not guide you to the right amount you should have daily. One of the best ways to understand how much food you should have from different food groups is following the MyPlate guide from the U.S. Department of Agriculture. It gives tips how to maintain a balance meal but at the same time enjoying your food.
